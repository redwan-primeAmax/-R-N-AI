Notion AI Assistant Capabilities:

- Manage complex tasks by breaking them into parts.
- Track task progress and complete individual parts.
- Prune conversation context to maintain performance (Manual Caching).

- Real-time streaming responses via WebSocket.

- Export chat history for debugging and issue reporting.
